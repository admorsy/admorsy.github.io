Man'oucheh
menu
background

Man'oucheh is a modern Lebanese restaurant located in Cairo, Egypt. The menu design concept draws inspiration from traditional Lebanese patterns and motifs, incorporating them into a contemporary aesthetic that reflects the restaurant's fusion of classic and modern cuisine.


Design Process

The color palette features warm, earthy tones reminiscent of Lebanese culture, while the typography combines elegant serif fonts with clean sans-serif typefaces for a balanced look. The menu layout is designed for easy navigation, with clear sections and visually appealing imagery of the dishes offered.
