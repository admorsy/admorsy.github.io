Vint is a Cairo-based brand that creates inspirational, design-led home accessories by combining vintage aesthetics with meaningful messaging. Its products—primarily coasters and cushions—feature retro color palettes, understated tones, and carefully composed layouts that stand apart from more common modern or highly colorful styles. Central to Vint’s identity is its use of thoughtful quotes, often drawn from well-known figures, which are integrated into the designs to bring moments of reflection, motivation, and positivity into everyday environments.

Rather than being purely decorative, Vint’s products are intended to subtly influence daily experiences by placing art and ideas into functional objects. A key example is the coaster, which becomes both a visual focal point and a medium for repeated engagement with uplifting messages. Through this approach, Vint positions its work at the intersection of design and lifestyle—offering items that not only enhance the aesthetic of a space but also contribute to a more intentional and inspiring atmosphere.


Design Process

The design process at Vint begins with the quote itself—carefully selecting or scanning for something inspirational, memorable, and visually suggestive. From there, the quote becomes the foundation for the entire concept, guiding the exploration of color palettes, visual forms, and typographic styles that reflect its tone and message. In some cases, designs also incorporate illustrated or stylized portraits of the figures behind the quotes, creating a connection with younger audiences through recognizable cultural icons. The greatest challenge lies in the arrangement of typography: ensuring the quote remains clear, prominent, and easily readable from a distance while still feeling visually engaging. Supporting elements such as shapes, textures, and imagery are then introduced with restraint, complementing the text without distracting from it. Once finalized, the design is adapted across different formats—such as coasters, cushions, and wall art—while preserving its clarity and visual impact.
